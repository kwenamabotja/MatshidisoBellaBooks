About Matshidiso Bella

Matshidiso Bella born Matshidiso Bella Makoea is an author hailing from Ga-Makweya, a laid back village in Moletjie, Limpopo. She writes sexy & cute books for young adults & teens centered around romance. Her books are easy to get lost into as they take you on an adventure out of this ordinary world. Her works includes her infamous The Pierce brother series: The cleaner, Breathe Again & Reckless behavior. One of her books The Swan was once a topic of discussion for three months on the famous Thobela FM radio station. When she's not writing, she's either reading, editing books for other authors or watching sexy vampires on TV. She started writing at age of 13 and has never looked back.
